Understanding the Key Differences

What Does an Architect Do?

Architects in Dehradun city focus on the structural and functional aspects of buildings. At Urbanways, our architectural team handles:

  • Structural Design: Creating the bones of your building
  • Space Planning: Optimizing floor plans and room layouts
  • Building Codes: Ensuring compliance with Dehradun Municipal Corporation regulations
  • Site Analysis: Evaluating terrain, especially important in Dehradun's hilly areas
  • Construction Documentation: Detailed drawings for builders
  • Project Management: Overseeing construction phases
  • Sustainability: Incorporating eco-friendly design for Uttarakhand's climate

When You Definitely Need an Architect:

  • Building a new home from scratch
  • Adding floors or extensions
  • Structural modifications (removing walls, changing rooflines)
  • Legal requirements for building permits
  • Projects requiring NOC from authorities

What Does an Interior Designer Do?

Interior designers in Dehradun at Urbanways transform existing spaces into functional, beautiful environments:

  • Space Optimization: Making the most of available area
  • Aesthetic Planning: Color schemes, materials, textures
  • Furniture Design: Custom and modular solutions
  • Lighting Design: Ambient, task, and accent lighting
  • Material Selection: Flooring, wall treatments, fabrics
  • Decor Coordination: Accessories, artwork, soft furnishings
  • Project Execution: Managing vendors and installation

When You Need an Interior Designer:

  • Renovating existing spaces
  • Updating home aesthetics
  • Furniture and decor selection
  • Kitchen and bathroom makeovers
  • Creating cohesive design themes
  • Maximizing storage solutions
